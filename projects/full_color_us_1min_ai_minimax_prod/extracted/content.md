THE CORE CONFLICT — BEYOND "FUNCTIONAL FATIGUE"
•Consumer Pain Point: The "White Fatigue" Phenomenon
•Data Insight:  Traditional laundry products are suffering from extreme visual stagnation. Quantitative results show that any colored powder 
generates significantly higher trial interest than standard white.
Quali research - "White [powder] that people are tired of looking at" ( 看腻了的白色 ).
•Demand Evolution: From "Function -Driven" to "Self -Pleasing"
•Data Insight:  Younger demographics (ages 18 –34) are the primary drivers of this shift. They no longer view laundry as just a cleaning task bu t as 
an emotional ritual , - "Self -Pleasing Experiences" ( 悦己体验 ).
•Economic Evidence: The Price Premium for Emotion
•Data Insight:  The qualitative and quantitative synergy confirms that "cleanliness is the 
entrance ticket, but emotion is the premium ticket."
•Willingness to Pay (WTP):  Colored laundry powder commands a measurable price premium of 
10% to 20% compared to traditional white powder.
"Darker shades... signify stronger efficacy and longer -lasting fragrance" .
Within the acceptable chromatic range, deeper saturation directly translates to 
higher functional credibility.

CONJOINT ANALYSIS & STRATEGIC WINNING MODEL
Conjoint analysis reveals the "Trade -off" logic of consumers when evaluating multiple attributes simultaneously. While individua l preferences vary, the 
"Purple -Lilac" configuration emerges as the undisputed market leader in the competitive landscape.
Attribute Importance Ranking (The Decision Drivers)
Data shows that Price remains the dominant factor, but Color -Fragrance and Packaging together 
contribute over 43% of the total decision weight.
•Price ( 45%): The primary gatekeeper for purchase.
•Color -Fragrance ( 25.5 %): The core differentiator for emotional value.
•Packaging ( 17.8 %): A critical visual touchpoint for brand perception.
•Functionality ( 11.7 %): Basic performance expectations.
Optimal "Golden Combination" Model
By selecting the top -performing levels from each attribute, we identified the winning concept:
•Color -Fragrance:  Purple -Lilac ( 40% Preference)  — Outperforming Blue -Ocean ( 30%), Green -
Tea ( 18%), and White -Jasmine ( 13%).
•Packaging:  Translucent ( 83% Preference)  — Significantly preferred over opaque options 
(17%).
•Function:  Long -lasting Fragrance ( 37% Preference)  — The highest -rated functional benefit.
•Price Point:  CNY 25/2kg (36% Preference)  — The "Sweet Spot" for maximum conversion.
